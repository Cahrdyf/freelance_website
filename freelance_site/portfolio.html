An Author's Website

Challenge

Suppose a client is a writer that wants to have their own website where they can add author information, showcase books, and their contact details.

Action

I made a responsive website where the client's readers can visit to learn more about them. This is a single-page site where the user may scroll down to see various details about their author.

This includes an "about me" section, an interactive, side-scrolling "book shelf" that shows their novels and other literature and a page that has contact information for the writer.


Result

Now both writer and reader can communicate with each other easier through the author's personal website. Furthermore, once the site is deployed, with the writer's presence on the internet made more prominent, they will be able to reach much more potential readers.
